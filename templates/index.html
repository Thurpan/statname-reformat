Statname Reformat

What it does
  • Finds lines labelled SH, HU, or stakes like 50, 100, 1K/2K.
  • Outputs exactly: 
    6m {bb/100}bb/100 over {Hands} hands
HU {bb/100}bb/100 over {Hands} hands
--
{Stake}NL {bb/100}bb/100 over {Hands} hands
{yyyymmdd}
  • Only includes stake lines where Hands > 10,000.
  • Understands hands like 193k, 8.1k, 2.5m. Ignores header rows and tolerates tabs/spaces and repeated headers.
  • Rounds bb/100 to 1 decimal (trims trailing .0). If input is empty, output is blank. Result is auto-copied to clipboard.
