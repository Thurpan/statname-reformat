Statname Reformat

What it does
  • Finds lines labelled SH, HU, or stakes like 50, 100, 1K/2K.
  • Outputs: 
    6m {bb/100}bb/100 over {Hands} hands
HU {bb/100}bb/100 over {Hands} hands
--
{Stake}NL {bb/100}bb/100 over {Hands} hands
{yyyymmdd}
  • Includes stake lines only when Hands > 10,000. Ignores headers. Rounds bb/100 to 1 decimal. Auto-copies after Format; you can also click Copy.
